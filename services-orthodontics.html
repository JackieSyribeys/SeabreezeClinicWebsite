Restorative Procedures

Crowns

A dental crown acts as a protective, custom-fitted "cap" that completely covers a damaged or weakened tooth above the gumline. This restoration completely reinstates the tooth's original shape, structural strength, and natural appearance.

Bridges

A dental bridge is a permanent restoration used to replace one or more missing teeth by bridging the gap between your remaining natural teeth. It utilizes custom prosthetic crowns anchored securely to the healthy teeth on either side to seamlessly restore your bite and smile symmetry.

Fillings

Dental fillings are used to repair teeth that have been structurally compromised by minor fractures or localized decay. After the damaged portion of the tooth is cleanly removed, a durable, tooth-colored composite material is bonded in place to seal the space and protect against future cavities.
