Surgical Procedures

Root Canals

When the internal tissue or nerve of a tooth becomes infected or inflamed, a root canal is performed to save the natural tooth. We use advanced techniques to ensure the treatment is comfortable, alleviating pain and protecting your smile from further complications.

Extractions

While preserving natural teeth is always our goal, sometimes an extraction is necessary to protect your overall oral environment. Whether dealing with severe decay, structural fracture, or impacted wisdom teeth, our gentle extraction methods prioritize your comfort and smooth recovery.

Dental Implants

Dental implants provide the most permanent, stable, and natural-feeling alternative for replacing missing teeth. Dr. Syribeys works comprehensively to place and restore implants, restoring both absolute chewing function and confident smile symmetry.
